STATISTICS & DATA SCIENCE (SDS) PHD PROGRAM


The Statistics and Data Science (SDS) PhD Program at the Marshall School of Business in the University of Southern California is hosted by the Department of Data Sciences and Operations (DSO).

Application Portal & Deadlines: To apply our Statistics & Data Science Ph.D. program, please visit the USC Marshall graduate admission website: www.marshall.usc.edu/programs/phd-program/admissions; The application deadline is 15th December 2023.

Information Sessions: We will be holding two information session over zoom where we will discuss details about the program and what makes it unique, the application process, and answer any questions you may have. The sessions are scheduled at:

  • October 25th, 2023: 4:00 - 5:00 PM PST (Zoom)
  • November 15th, 2023: 5:00 - 6:20 PM PST (In person and over zoom)

There are also other general information sessions. Click on the link below if you want to attend one of these sessions: Click here.
